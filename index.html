From Virtual Assistance to UX/UI Design

Hello, I'm Rosmary. For four years, my role as a Virtual Assistant taught me the importance of efficiency and clear communication.

This experience was my bridge to UX/UI design. I discovered that my ability to organize and empathize are the same skills that define great design.

Now I combine discipline and creativity to design user-friendly digital products that solve real problems.
